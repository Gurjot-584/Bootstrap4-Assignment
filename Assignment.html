About Our Cinema

Located in beautiful Portland, Oregon’s Pearl District, Academy Cinemas brings movie lovers an unforgettable big-screen experience.

We are passionate about making every visit enjoyable, from premium sound systems to extra-wide plush recliners.

Our auditoriums are modern, comfortable, and disability-friendly so everyone can enjoy the magic of cinema.
